Study Information

You will complete two versions of the Stroop task (a classic text version and a game-like version). You'll identify the ink color of words as quickly and accurately as possible. Total time: ~10-15 minutes.

Before You Begin

  • Quiet place: choose a calm, distraction-free environment. Silence your phone and close other apps/tabs.
  • One sitting: complete all parts in a single session without breaks to keep results consistent.
  • Do not navigate away: do not click the browser's Back button or refresh/close the tab. This will erase your progress.
Warning: Using the browser's Back/Refresh will reset the task and delete your progress.

Data & Privacy

We collect task responses (accuracy, reaction times) and a participant ID you provide. Data are stored de-identified for research purposes.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty.
